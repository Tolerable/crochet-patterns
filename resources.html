Free Crochet Resources

The best places to find free patterns online

Free Pattern Databases

  • Ravelry - Free Crochet Patterns

    Massive database with thousands of free patterns. Filter by project type, yarn weight, difficulty, and more.

  • AllFreeCrochet

    Huge collection of free patterns for every skill level. Great for beginners!

  • Yarnspirations

    Free patterns from major yarn brands. High quality with detailed instructions.

  • Repeat Crafter Me

    Adorable amigurumi and kids' projects. Famous for cute animal patterns!

Pokemon & Character Patterns

  • Ravelry - Pokemon Patterns

    Community-shared Pokemon amigurumi patterns. All free!

  • 1 Dog Woof - Amigurumi

    Cute character patterns including video game and anime inspired designs.

  • Amigurumi Today

    Daily free amigurumi patterns. Toys, animals, characters.

Learn to Crochet

  • Bella Coco - YouTube

    Excellent video tutorials for beginners. Clear and easy to follow.

  • TL Yarn Crafts - YouTube

    Modern crochet tutorials, tips, and pattern walkthroughs.

  • The Spruce Crafts - Crochet Basics

    Written guides for all the basic stitches with photos.

Stitch Guides & References

  • Craft Yarn Council - Chart Symbols

    Official crochet chart symbols reference. Essential for reading patterns.

  • Yarn Weight Standards

    Understanding yarn weights from lace to jumbo.
