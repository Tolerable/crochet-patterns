Crocheted Beanie with Fluffy Ears & Mane

This cozy Eevee beanie features the signature large pointed ears with cream inner coloring and a fluffy mane around the brim. Perfect for any Pokemon trainer!

Materials Needed:

  • Brown worsted weight yarn (#4) - ~250 yards
  • Cream/tan yarn (ear inner, mane) - ~100 yards
  • Black yarn (nose, optional eyes) - ~10 yards
  • 5.0mm (H/8) crochet hook
  • Yarn needle
  • Stitch marker
  • Polyfill stuffing (for ears)

Gauge (Important!):

14 sc x 16 rows = 4" (10cm)
Check your gauge! If too loose, go down a hook size. Too tight, go up.
Hat should have 1-2" negative ease for snug fit.

Sizing:

Adult (20-22" head): Follow pattern as written
Teen (19-20"): Stop increases at Rnd 6 (72 sts)
Child (18-19"): Stop increases at Rnd 5 (60 sts)
Toddler (16-18"): Stop increases at Rnd 4 (48 sts)

Abbreviations:

sc = single crochet | inc = increase (2 sc in 1 st) | dec = invisible decrease | hdc = half double crochet | sl st = slip stitch | ch = chain | MR = magic ring | BLO = back loop only

BEANIE BODY (Brown)

Work in continuous rounds, use stitch marker.

Rnd 1: 6 sc in MR (6)
Rnd 2: inc around (12)
Rnd 3: [sc, inc] x6 (18)
Rnd 4: [2 sc, inc] x6 (24)
Rnd 5: [3 sc, inc] x6 (30)
Rnd 6: [4 sc, inc] x6 (36)
Rnd 7: [5 sc, inc] x6 (42)
Rnd 8: [6 sc, inc] x6 (48)
Rnd 9: [7 sc, inc] x6 (54)
Rnd 10: [8 sc, inc] x6 (60)
Rnd 11: [9 sc, inc] x6 (66)
Rnd 12: [10 sc, inc] x6 (72)
Rnd 13: [11 sc, inc] x6 (78)
Rnd 14: [12 sc, inc] x6 (84)

Crown complete! Now work even:

Rnd 15-30: sc around (84) - 16 rounds
Or until beanie reaches ~7.5" from crown

Rnd 31: sc in BLO around (84) - creates ridge
Rnd 32-34: sc around (84) - 3 rounds
Fasten off, weave in ends.

EEVEE EARS (Make 2)

Ears are large and pointed with cream inner.

Outer Ear (Brown) - Make 2:
Ch 5.
Row 1: sc in 2nd ch from hook, sc across (4)
Row 2: ch 1, turn, inc, 2 sc, inc (6)
Row 3: ch 1, turn, inc, 4 sc, inc (8)
Row 4: ch 1, turn, inc, 6 sc, inc (10)
Row 5: ch 1, turn, inc, 8 sc, inc (12)
Row 6: ch 1, turn, inc, 10 sc, inc (14)
Row 7-10: ch 1, turn, sc across (14) - 4 rows
Row 11: ch 1, turn, dec, 10 sc, dec (12)
Row 12: ch 1, turn, dec, 8 sc, dec (10)
Fasten off.

Inner Ear (Cream) - Make 2:
Ch 4.
Row 1: sc in 2nd ch from hook, sc across (3)
Row 2: ch 1, turn, inc, sc, inc (5)
Row 3: ch 1, turn, inc, 3 sc, inc (7)
Row 4: ch 1, turn, inc, 5 sc, inc (9)
Row 5-7: ch 1, turn, sc across (9) - 3 rows
Row 8: ch 1, turn, dec, 5 sc, dec (7)
Fasten off.

Sew cream inner to brown outer, fold and sew base closed.

FLUFFY MANE (Cream)

Attached to the brim ridge for Eevee's signature fluff!

Method 1 - Loop Stitch Mane:
Join cream yarn at brim ridge (Rnd 31).
Work loop stitch around entire brim:
- Insert hook, wrap yarn around finger 2x
- Pull both loops through, yarn over, pull through all
- Continue around (84 loops)
Fasten off.

Method 2 - Fringe Mane (Easier):
Cut 84 pieces of cream yarn, each 6" long.
Fold each piece in half.
Use crochet hook to pull folded loop through brim ridge.
Pull yarn ends through loop, tighten.
Repeat around entire brim.
Trim to desired fluffiness (~2" long).

Brush fringe with a pet brush for extra fluff!

ASSEMBLY

Ear Placement:
- Position ears about 4" apart on top of beanie
- Eevee's ears point UP and slightly outward
- Sew securely around the base, stuff lightly before closing

Optional Face:
- Eyes: Black safety eyes (15mm) or embroidered
- Nose: Small black triangle
- Place face features centered, about 3" above mane

Pro Tips:

  • Use a warm chocolate brown - not too dark!
  • Cream yarn should match Eevee's collar ruff color
  • Brush the mane fringe for authentic fluffy look
  • Ears should be large - about 4-5" tall
  • Stuff ears lightly so they stand up
  • For Eeveelutions, just change the colors!
