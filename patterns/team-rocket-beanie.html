Crocheted Beanie with "R" Emblem

Here's your Team Rocket beanie pattern - perfect for any aspiring Pokemon villain!

Materials Needed:

  • White worsted weight yarn (#4) - ~200 yards
  • Red yarn (R emblem) - ~30 yards
  • Black yarn (optional trim) - ~20 yards
  • 5.0mm (H/8) crochet hook
  • Yarn needle
  • Stitch marker

Gauge (Important!):

14 sc x 16 rows = 4" (10cm)
Check your gauge! If too loose, go down a hook size. Too tight, go up.
Hat should have 1-2" negative ease (smaller than head) for snug fit.

Sizing:

Adult (20-22" head): Follow pattern as written
Teen (19-20"): Stop increases at Rnd 6 (72 sts)
Child (18-19"): Stop increases at Rnd 5 (60 sts)

Abbreviations:

sc = single crochet | inc = increase (2 sc in 1 st) | hdc = half double crochet | sl st = slip stitch | ch = chain | MR = magic ring | BLO = back loop only

BEANIE BODY (White)

Work in continuous rounds, use stitch marker.

Rnd 1: 6 sc in MR (6)
Rnd 2: inc around (12)
Rnd 3: [sc, inc] x6 (18)
Rnd 4: [2 sc, inc] x6 (24)
Rnd 5: [3 sc, inc] x6 (30)
Rnd 6: [4 sc, inc] x6 (36)
Rnd 7: [5 sc, inc] x6 (42)
Rnd 8: [6 sc, inc] x6 (48)
Rnd 9: [7 sc, inc] x6 (54)
Rnd 10: [8 sc, inc] x6 (60)
Rnd 11: [9 sc, inc] x6 (66)
Rnd 12: [10 sc, inc] x6 (72)
Rnd 13: [11 sc, inc] x6 (78)
Rnd 14: [12 sc, inc] x6 (84)

Crown complete! Now work even:

Rnd 15-30: sc around (84) - 16 rounds
Or until beanie reaches ~7.5" from crown

Rnd 31: sc in BLO around (84) - creates ridge for brim
Rnd 32-34: sc around (84) - 3 rounds
Fasten off, weave in ends.

RED "R" EMBLEM

Based on Hookok Letter R Pattern - adapted for Team Rocket.

Step 1 - Main Stem (Red):
Ch 19.
Row 1: sc in 2nd ch from hook, sc across (18)
Row 2: ch 1, turn, sc across (18)
Row 3: ch 1, turn, sc across (18)
Fasten off.

Step 2 - Curved Top:
Join red yarn, skip 9 sts, ch 12.
Skip 5, sl st 2.
In chain: 2 sc, inc, sc, hdc, inc x2, sc, inc, 2 sc
Skip 1, sl st 2, 5 sc, inc x5, 5 sc, sl st.

Step 3 - Diagonal Leg:
Ch 9, connect with 2 sl st.
In chain: 8 sc, inc, turn
Ch 1, 10 sc, sl st 2, turn
9 sc, inc. Fasten off.

Sew onto front center of beanie, about 2" above brim.

Easier Alternative:

Cut an "R" from red felt (about 3" tall) and use fabric glue or hand-stitch it on. This gives a cleaner look and is much faster! You can find Team Rocket R templates on DeviantArt.

OPTIONAL: BLACK TRIM

Join black yarn at brim ridge (Rnd 31).
Sl st around the ridge for a clean black line.
Or: Add 2 rounds of black sc at very bottom of brim.

Pro Tips:

  • Use stitch markers to track round beginnings
  • Try on as you go to check fit before the brim
  • For a cleaner "R", use felt and fabric glue instead of crochet
