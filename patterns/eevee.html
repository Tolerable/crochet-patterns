About This Pattern

Create an adorable fluffy Eevee! This normal-type Pokemon is a fan favorite and perfect for collectors. The fluffy mane and tail make this a standout piece.

Skill Level: Intermediate

Finished Size: Approximately 7 inches tall

Materials Needed

  • Brown worsted weight yarn (main body) - approx 60g
  • Cream/tan yarn (mane, tail tip) - approx 30g
  • Dark brown yarn (eyes, ears) - small amount
  • Black yarn (nose, mouth) - small amount
  • 4.0mm (G/6) crochet hook
  • Fiberfill stuffing
  • Safety eyes (15mm black)
  • Yarn needle, stitch markers

Pattern Instructions

HEAD (Brown)

Rnd 1: 6 sc in MR (6)
Rnd 2: inc around (12)
Rnd 3: [sc, inc] x6 (18)
Rnd 4: [2 sc, inc] x6 (24)
Rnd 5: [3 sc, inc] x6 (30)
Rnd 6: [4 sc, inc] x6 (36)
Rnd 7: [5 sc, inc] x6 (42)
Rnd 8-15: sc around (42) - 8 rounds
Rnd 16: [5 sc, dec] x6 (36)
Rnd 17: [4 sc, dec] x6 (30)
Rnd 18: [3 sc, dec] x6 (24)
Insert safety eyes between Rnd 11-12, 10 sts apart. Stuff.
Rnd 19: [2 sc, dec] x6 (18)
Rnd 20: [sc, dec] x6 (12)
Rnd 21: dec around (6). Close.

BODY (Brown)

Rnd 1: 6 sc in MR (6)
Rnd 2: inc around (12)
Rnd 3: [sc, inc] x6 (18)
Rnd 4: [2 sc, inc] x6 (24)
Rnd 5: [3 sc, inc] x6 (30)
Rnd 6-13: sc around (30) - 8 rounds
Rnd 14: [3 sc, dec] x6 (24)
Rnd 15-16: sc around (24)
Rnd 17: [2 sc, dec] x6 (18)
Stuff firmly. Leave tail for sewing.

EARS (Make 2) - Brown with Dark Brown inside

Outer ear (BROWN):
Rnd 1: 4 sc in MR (4)
Rnd 2: [sc, inc] x2 (6)
Rnd 3: [2 sc, inc] x2 (8)
Rnd 4: [3 sc, inc] x2 (10)
Rnd 5: [4 sc, inc] x2 (12)
Rnd 6-8: sc around (12)

Inner ear (DARK BROWN): Same as outer but stop at Rnd 5.
Sew inner to outer, then attach to head.

FLUFFY MANE (Cream) - Make loops around head

Using cream yarn, create a "loop stitch" collar around neck:

Ch 30, join to form ring.
Rnd 1: sc around (30)
Rnd 2-4: *sc, loop st* around (30 loops)
Loop stitch: Insert hook, wrap yarn around finger 2x, pull through, complete sc.

Sew mane around neck, fluffing loops outward.

TAIL (Brown with Cream tip)

With BROWN:
Rnd 1: 6 sc in MR (6)
Rnd 2: inc around (12)
Rnd 3: [sc, inc] x6 (18)
Rnd 4-8: sc around (18) - 5 rounds
Change to CREAM:
Rnd 9-12: sc around (18) - 4 rounds
Rnd 13: [sc, dec] x6 (12)
Rnd 14: Use loop stitch all around (12 loops)
Stuff lightly. Sew fluffy tip.

LEGS (Make 4) - Brown

Rnd 1: 6 sc in MR (6)
Rnd 2: inc around (12)
Rnd 3-6: sc around (12) - 4 rounds
Rnd 7: [2 sc, dec] x3 (9)
Stuff. Leave tail for sewing.

Eevee Tips

  • The mane is key - make it fluffy!
  • Brush out the loop stitches with a pet brush for extra fluff
  • Embroider a small triangle nose in black
  • Add a tiny curved smile below the nose
  • Ears should point slightly outward and up
