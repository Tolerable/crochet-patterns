Crocheted Beanie with Curl, Ears & Big Eyes

This sweet Jigglypuff beanie features the signature forehead curl, cute pointed ears, and those iconic big eyes. Don't forget to sing!

Materials Needed:

  • Pink worsted weight yarn (#4) - ~250 yards
  • White yarn (eyes) - ~20 yards
  • Teal/green yarn (eye color) - ~10 yards
  • Black yarn (pupils, details) - ~10 yards
  • 5.0mm (H/8) crochet hook
  • Yarn needle
  • Stitch marker
  • Polyfill stuffing (for curl)

Gauge (Important!):

14 sc x 16 rows = 4" (10cm)
Check your gauge! If too loose, go down a hook size. Too tight, go up.
Hat should have 1-2" negative ease for snug fit.

Sizing:

Adult (20-22" head): Follow pattern as written
Teen (19-20"): Stop increases at Rnd 6 (72 sts)
Child (18-19"): Stop increases at Rnd 5 (60 sts)
Toddler (16-18"): Stop increases at Rnd 4 (48 sts)

Abbreviations:

sc = single crochet | inc = increase (2 sc in 1 st) | dec = invisible decrease | sl st = slip stitch | ch = chain | MR = magic ring | BLO = back loop only

BEANIE BODY (Pink)

Work in continuous rounds, use stitch marker.

Rnd 1: 6 sc in MR (6)
Rnd 2: inc around (12)
Rnd 3: [sc, inc] x6 (18)
Rnd 4: [2 sc, inc] x6 (24)
Rnd 5: [3 sc, inc] x6 (30)
Rnd 6: [4 sc, inc] x6 (36)
Rnd 7: [5 sc, inc] x6 (42)
Rnd 8: [6 sc, inc] x6 (48)
Rnd 9: [7 sc, inc] x6 (54)
Rnd 10: [8 sc, inc] x6 (60)
Rnd 11: [9 sc, inc] x6 (66)
Rnd 12: [10 sc, inc] x6 (72)
Rnd 13: [11 sc, inc] x6 (78)
Rnd 14: [12 sc, inc] x6 (84)

Crown complete! Now work even:

Rnd 15-30: sc around (84) - 16 rounds
Or until beanie reaches ~7.5" from crown

Rnd 31: sc in BLO around (84) - creates ridge
Rnd 32-34: sc around (84) - 3 rounds
Fasten off, weave in ends.

SIGNATURE CURL (Pink)

The iconic forehead swirl!

Rnd 1: 5 sc in MR (5)
Rnd 2: inc around (10)
Rnd 3: [sc, inc] x5 (15)
Rnd 4-6: sc around (15) - 3 rounds
Rnd 7: [sc, dec] x5 (10)
Rnd 8-10: sc around (10) - 3 rounds
Rnd 11: [sc, dec] x3, sc (7)
Rnd 12-14: sc around (7) - 3 rounds
Fasten off, stuff lightly.

Shape into a curl/spiral when sewing to beanie.

POINTED EARS (Make 2 - Pink)

Small triangular ears.

Ch 3.
Row 1: sc in 2nd ch from hook, sc (2)
Row 2: ch 1, turn, inc x2 (4)
Row 3: ch 1, turn, inc, 2 sc, inc (6)
Row 4: ch 1, turn, inc, 4 sc, inc (8)
Row 5: ch 1, turn, inc, 6 sc, inc (10)
Row 6: ch 1, turn, sc across (10)
Fasten off, leave tail for sewing.

BIG EYES (Make 2)

Jigglypuff's signature big oval eyes.

White Outer (Make 2):
Ch 7.
Rnd 1: sc in 2nd ch from hook, 4 sc, 3 sc in last ch
(rotate to work other side) 4 sc, 2 sc in last (14)
Rnd 2: inc, 4 sc, inc x3, 4 sc, inc x2 (20)
Rnd 3: sc, inc, 4 sc, [sc, inc] x3, 4 sc, [sc, inc] x2 (26)
Sl st, fasten off.

Teal/Green Iris (Make 2):
Rnd 1: 6 sc in MR (6)
Rnd 2: inc around (12)
Sl st, fasten off.

Black Pupil (Make 2):
Rnd 1: 6 sc in MR (6)
Sl st, fasten off.

Layer: white base > teal iris > black pupil
Add a tiny white highlight stitch on each pupil!

ASSEMBLY

Curl Placement:
- Sew curl to center front, just above where eyes will go
- Curl should spiral/swirl to the side
- Position about 2" down from crown

Eye Placement:
- Place eyes on either side of center, below curl
- Space about 1.5" apart
- Position about 3-4" above brim

Ear Placement:
- Position on top of beanie, about 4" apart
- Angle slightly outward
- Jigglypuff ears are small and pointy

Pro Tips:

  • Use a bubblegum/light pink - Jigglypuff is pastel!
  • The curl is THE signature feature - take time to shape it nicely
  • Eyes should be big and placed close together
  • The white highlight on pupils makes them look alive
  • Ears are smaller than Pikachu's - don't make them too big
  • For felt version: cut eye pieces from felt and layer with fabric glue
