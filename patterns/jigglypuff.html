Jigglypuff Amigurumi

Normal/Fairy Type - #039

The adorable Balloon Pokemon who sings everyone to sleep!

"Jig-ga-ly-puff, Jig-ga-ly..." *everyone falls asleep*

Pattern Info

  • Difficulty: Easy - Great for beginners!
  • Finished Size: ~5 inches tall
  • Technique: Standard amigurumi
  • Special Feature: Two-color crocheted eyes

Materials Needed

  • Worsted weight yarn: Pink, Turquoise, White
  • 4.0mm (G) crochet hook
  • 12mm safety eyes OR crochet the eyes
  • Black felt for ear details
  • Stuffing & yarn needle
