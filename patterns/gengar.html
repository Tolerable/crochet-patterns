Gengar Amigurumi

Ghost/Poison Type - #094

The mischievous Shadow Pokemon with a wicked grin!

Pattern Info

  • Difficulty: Intermediate (lots of pieces!)
  • Finished Size: ~5-6 inches
  • Technique: Standard amigurumi with many sewn pieces
  • Special Features: Popcorn stitch spikes, finger details

Heads Up!

This pattern has a lot of sewing due to all the spikes on Gengar's back. Budget extra time for assembly! There's also a popcorn stitch alternative for the spikes.

Materials Needed

  • DK weight yarn: Purple (Violet) and White
  • 3.0mm crochet hook
  • 10mm or 12mm RED safety eyes
  • Stuffing & yarn needle
